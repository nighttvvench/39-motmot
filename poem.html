Our Love Poem

A Poem for Us

The days we’ve spent, like pages turned,
Each chapter more, each lesson learned.
With every laugh, every tear,
Our love has grown, year after year.

The stars above can never compare,
To the warmth and light we share.
Through every storm, and every fight,
We find our way back to the light.

You are the song that I hum in my heart,
The piece that was missing from the start.
In your eyes, I find my home,
No more wandering, never alone.

39 months, but it feels like forever,
Through every season, no matter the weather.
With you, love, I have found my way,
And I’ll love you more each passing day.
